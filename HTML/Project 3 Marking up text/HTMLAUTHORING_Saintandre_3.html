Yonkers, New York

Yonker is a large suburb of New York City that is home to multiple attractions.

Yonkers, New York is the fourth most populous city in the U.S., behind New York City, Buffalo, and Rochester.
The population of Yonkers was 195,976 as enumerated in the 2010 United States Census and is estimated to have increased by 2.5% to 200,807 in 2016.[6]
It is an inner suburb of New York City, directly to the north of the Bronx and approximately two miles (3 km) north of the northernmost point in Manhattan.

Yonkers' downtown is centered on a plaza known as Getty Square, where the municipal government is located.
The downtown area also houses significant local businesses and non-profits, and serves as a major retail hub for Yonkers and the northwest Bronx.
